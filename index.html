CLASS STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES

Retrieved from https://levelskip.com/strategy/Axie-Infinity-Classes-Guide

BUFFS

Speed Up

Increases Axies Speed by 20%. For a designated time. Can stack.

Morale Up

Increases Axies Morale by 20% . For a designated time. Can stack.

Attack Up

Increases Axies Attack by 20% . Stays until next attack. Can stack.

DEBUFFS

Stun

Axie skips its next attack or defense. Stays until Axie tries to attack.

Aroma

Axie will draw all incoming attacks. For a designated time.

Chill

Axie cannot enter last stand. For a designated time.

Fear

Axie cannot attack. For a designated time.

Fragile

Shield takes 200% damage. For a designated time.

Jinx

Axie cannot score critical hits. For a designated time.

Lethal

Next hit on this Axie is always a critical strike. Stays until attacked.

Poison

Axie loses 3hp each turn taken (by either team). Can stack. Stays until its removed.

Sleep

Axie cannot defend next time it is hit. Stays until Axie tries to attack.

Stench

Axie is ignored when there are 2 or more targets. For a designated time.

Speed Down

Decreases Axies speed by 20%. For a designated time. Can Stack.

Morale Down

Decreases Axies morale by 20%. For a designated time. Can Stack.

Attack Down

Decreases Axies attack by 20%. Stays until next attack. Can Stack.
